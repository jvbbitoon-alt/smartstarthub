How do I receive my purchase after buying? ⌄
All products are instant digital downloads. After completing your Etsy purchase, the PDF is available immediately in your Etsy account under "Purchases and Reviews." You'll also get a download link in your order confirmation email. No physical item is shipped — you own the file forever.
What paper sizes are included? ⌄
Every download includes BOTH US Letter (8.5×11 inches) and A4 (210×297mm) in the same ZIP file — labeled clearly so you always know which is which. Whether you're in the Philippines, Australia, the UK, or the US, your files will print perfectly.
Can I print for my Sunday school class or co-op? ⌄
Yes! All SmartStartHub products are licensed for personal and classroom use. Print multiple copies for your own children, Sunday school class, homeschool co-op, or children's ministry — as many times as you need. The only restriction is reselling the files or sharing the digital download itself with others.
What do I need to print these at home? ⌄
Any standard inkjet or laser printer works perfectly. For most products, regular 20 lb copy paper is fine. For flashcards, 65 lb cardstock makes them more durable and easier for kids to handle. A standard home printer and basic paper is genuinely all you need.
Can I edit or customize the files? ⌄
Products are delivered as print-ready PDFs — they are not editable files. They're designed to be print-and-use with no editing needed. If you need a specific customization (like a name on the certificate), send us a message on Etsy and we'll see what we can do!
What is your refund policy? ⌄
Because these are instant digital downloads, we cannot accept returns once the file has been downloaded. However, if there's any issue with your file — corrupt download, missing pages, wrong file — message us immediately and we'll resolve it right away. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
Do you offer bundles or discounts? ⌄
Yes! Our Back-to-School Mega Bundle gives you 40% off the individual prices. We also run seasonal sales around Christmas, Easter, and Back-to-School. Follow our Etsy shop or join our email list (grab your free printable above!) to be the first to know about sales.
